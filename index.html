Your Beauty, Our Passion

Welcome to Elegance Beauty Parlour, your premier destination for luxury beauty treatments and personalized care. We stay ahead of industry trends, continuously updating our skills and techniques to offer you the latest innovations in beauty care. From traditional treatments to cutting-edge procedures, we use only premium, dermatologically-tested products that are gentle on your skin and deliver exceptional results.

At Elegance, we believe that true beauty comes from confidence. That's why we take the time to understand your unique needs, preferences, and lifestyle to create customized beauty solutions that enhance your natural features and reflect your personal style.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our services and policies

Do I need to book an appointment in advance?

While walk-ins are welcome, we highly recommend booking an appointment in advance to ensure availability and minimize wait times. You can book online through our contact form, call us directly, or visit our salon. For bridal packages and special treatments, advance booking is mandatory.

What products do you use?

We exclusively use premium, dermatologically-tested products from internationally renowned brands. All our products are carefully selected for their quality, safety, and effectiveness. We also offer organic and natural product options for clients with sensitive skin or specific preferences.

How long does a typical facial treatment take?

A standard facial treatment typically takes 60-75 minutes, including consultation, cleansing, exfoliation, massage, mask application, and moisturizing. Specialized treatments like anti-aging or deep cleansing facials may take up to 90 minutes. We recommend arriving 10 minutes early for your first visit to complete any necessary paperwork.

What is your cancellation policy?

We understand that plans change. Please notify us at least 24 hours in advance if you need to cancel or reschedule your appointment. Late cancellations or no-shows may be subject to a cancellation fee. For bridal packages, we require 48 hours notice for cancellations.

Are your services suitable for sensitive skin?

Absolutely! We offer specialized treatments and products specifically designed for sensitive skin. During your consultation, please inform our specialists about any skin sensitivities, allergies, or concerns. We can perform patch tests before treatments and customize our services to ensure your comfort and safety.

Do you offer gift certificates?

Yes! Gift certificates are available for any amount or specific service. They make perfect gifts for birthdays, anniversaries, Mother's Day, or any special occasion. Gift certificates are valid for one year from the date of purchase and can be used for any of our services or packages.
